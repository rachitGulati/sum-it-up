If you Love Reading Collecting Highlighting

A simple chrome extension for all of your needs. For Mini Bookmarking on the pages

Jump it

You can jump within the page on the highlighted content or to the different page on the other website.

Tag it

You can tag your content for the better segregation of data. It will help you in future searching.

Search it

You can search by Date, Text or Tags from the top nav search Bar.
